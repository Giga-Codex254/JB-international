About JB International

Founded in 2018 in Nairobi, Kenya, JB International is a multinational corporation focused on import/export, industrial and commercial mining of precious metals, food sales, brand development, and strategic consulting services.

Import/Export

We are committed to ethical sourcing, sustainable logistics, and fair trade partnerships.

  • Strategic hubs in Mombasa, Miami, and Dubai
  • Air, land, and sea freight services
  • Bonded, insured, and fully licensed operations
  • Real-time tracking and supply chain transparency

Precious Metals

We specialize in exploration, mining, processing and distribution of precious metals.

  • Land restoration and sustainability practices
  • Secure and ethical supply chains
  • Local community employment and training initiatives

Food Sales

We supply wholesale food and beverages to restaurants, markets, and hotels globally.

  • Avocados, cashews, and other organic produce
  • Livestock and poultry farming
  • Single-origin African coffees and teas

Brand Development & Consulting

We help you craft a unique voice and image that resonates with your audience.

  • Logo and identity design
  • Website creation
  • Social media & influencer marketing
  • Event planning & media management
